📍 Google Maps Business Analysis Dashboard – Surat

An interactive Streamlit dashboard analyzing 691 local businesses in Surat, scraped from Google Maps, to identify digital presence gaps and business opportunities.

Pages


🏠 Dashboard – Key metrics (businesses, categories, areas, ratings, website stats)
📊 Business Overview – Top categories, areas, and website distribution charts
🎯 Opportunity Analysis – High-rating businesses without a website, by category/area
💡 Business Insights – Written summary of key findings


Key Findings


535/691 businesses (77.42%) have no website
Average rating: 4.35/5
448 businesses identified as opportunities (high rating + no website)
Top areas: Palanpur Canal Road, Rander Road, Gujarat Gas Circle


Tech Stack

Python · Streamlit · Pandas · Plotly Express
